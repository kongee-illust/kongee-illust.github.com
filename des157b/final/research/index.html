What Food Tells Us About Culture

Le, Chau B. “What Food Tells Us about Culture.” Freely Magazine, 7 Jan. 2017, https://freelymagazine.com/2017/01/07/what-food-tells-us-about-culture/.

This article discusses the significance of food in different cultures. The author states that the food we eat in our families become a part of our identity as well as an embodiment of the culture and heritage we come from. According to the author, making food from our culture symbolizes pride and celebrates diversity. This is a great article for me to research to figure out the KPI for this project, which is a majority of the users feeling proud of their culture and family and learning about others’ culture through the communal posts.

7 UX best practices for designing your blog posts in 2021

“7 UX Best Practices for Designing Your Blog Posts in 2021.” Icons8 Blog, 11 Aug. 2021, https://blog.icons8.com/articles/7-ux-best-practices-for-designing-your-blog-posts-in-2021/.

This article explains the importance of UX in designing a blog post. I wanted to use this source to evaluate some key features and design elements to include in my blog to maximize the user experience. Some takeaways from the blog include establishing a visual hierarchy, using interactive elements, and encouraging engagement. This article helped me view the project and its design from a designer’s point of view to retain users and maximize interactions.

American Identity; the Lost Identity of Asian American Teens

Wong, Olivia, and Jocelyn Wu. “American Identity; the Lost Identity of Asian American Teens.” ISSIA Magazine, 3 July 2020, https://issiahk.org/blog/american-identity-the-lost-identity-of-asian-american-teens-.html.

This article talks tells the stories of two Asian American women and their journey of navigating their identity as they assimilate into the popular culture in the United States. This article is useful for me to identify different type of information that people can empathize with. As it helped me empathize with people in my community and helped me think of my own, this article is useful in helping me establish the importance of storytelling in our community.
