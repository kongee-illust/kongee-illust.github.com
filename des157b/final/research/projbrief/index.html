FINAL PROJECT—

project brief.

summary, goals, strategies, goals.

summary

My project focuses on shining a light on the diversity of the Asian & Asian American student body at UCD through shared stories of culture, practices, and unique backgrounds through food, a love language for many households that carry heartwarming stories.

project goals

Through my project, I hope to generate a sense of solidarity among the Asian & Asian American students at UCD, provide an opportunity for them to recount on nostalgic memories, and reinforce their identity through storytelling.

design strategies

To draw the most focus on the actual content of the website, I plan on having an overall minimalistic design accompanied by one or two highlight colors for information hierarchy. For this purpose of effective information display, I plan on coming up with a design guidelines page to ensure consistency across all screens in this project.

interaction strategies

As stated above, my biggest goal for effective UX in this project is to minimize unnecessary elements that may distract the user from the actual content. However, given that interactions are indispensable in a smooth user experience, some interactions I’m thinking of are hover animations inspired by my competitive analysis, loading animations, and smooth scroll functions.

experience goals

Through the strategies outlined above, I hope my project, its function, and contents help the Asian & Asian American community remind themselves of their roots, while celebrating the diversity and unique stories from each other. I hope to have the navigation experience feel as if the user is reading various stories!
